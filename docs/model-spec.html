06 · Expected results

What the numbers will actually look like

Two things dominate real performance: the severe class imbalance in the training data, and whether you tune thresholds. Both are shown here with the deltas you can expect.

Training-set class frequency → per-class difficulty

Recall tracks frequency. Expect strong performance on the green classes and low, high-variance recall on the red ones — this is exactly why the model uses focal loss, inverse-frequency weighting, and per-class thresholds. (√-scaled bars; actual counts at right.)
